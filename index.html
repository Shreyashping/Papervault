About Us

About PaperVault

PaperVault is a free, community-driven archive of past exam papers, sample papers, and practice questions for Indian students. Our mission is simple: make quality study material accessible to every student, regardless of background or location.

What We Offer

We cover all major Indian exams including Class 10 and Class 12 Boards (CBSE and State), JEE Mains, JEE Advanced, and NEET — with Past Papers, Sample Papers, Practice Questions, and an interactive Quiz Zone.

Our Mission

Every year, millions of Indian students prepare for board exams and entrance tests. Access to previous year papers is one of the most effective study strategies, yet many students struggle to find them in one organised place. PaperVault solves that — for free, forever.
