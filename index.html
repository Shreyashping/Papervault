About Us

About PaperVault

PaperVault is a free, community-driven archive of past exam papers, sample papers, and practice questions for Indian students. Our mission is simple: make quality study material accessible to every student, regardless of their background or location.

What We Offer

We cover all major Indian exams including Class 10 and Class 12 Boards (CBSE and State), JEE Mains, JEE Advanced, and NEET. For each exam, we provide three types of material:

  • Past Papers — Real previous year question papers
  • Sample Papers — Official model papers released by boards
  • Practice Questions — Topic-wise practice material

Our Mission

Every year, millions of Indian students prepare for board exams and entrance tests. Access to previous year papers is one of the most effective study strategies, yet many students struggle to find them in one organised place. PaperVault solves that problem — for free, forever.
